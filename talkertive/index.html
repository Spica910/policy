Talkertive Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Talkertive ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our English conversation practice application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Voice Data

  • What: Voice recordings during conversation practice
  • How: Through your device microphone when you press the speak button
  • Processing: Voice data is processed using Google Speech Recognition or OpenAI Whisper API
  • Storage: Voice data is NOT stored permanently. It is processed in real-time and immediately discarded

2.2 Conversation Data

  • What: Text transcriptions of conversations and AI responses
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Duration: Until you close the app or clear the conversation

2.3 Settings and Preferences

  • Difficulty level
  • Speech speed
  • Topic preferences
  • API keys (encrypted and stored locally)

2.4 Advertising Data

  • Google AdMob: Collects advertising ID for personalized ads
  • Purpose: To show relevant advertisements
  • Opt-out: You can reset your advertising ID in device settings

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Voice Recognition: To convert your speech to text for conversation practice
  • AI Processing: To generate appropriate conversation responses
  • App Improvement: To enhance user experience and fix issues
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads (free version only)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Services

  • Speech Recognition: Processes voice input
  • Text-to-Speech: Generates spoken responses
  • AdMob: Displays advertisements
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Generates conversation responses
  • Data: Receives only text transcriptions, not voice data
  • Privacy Policy: Google AI Terms

4.3 OpenAI (Optional)

  • Purpose: Alternative AI response generation
  • Data: Receives only text transcriptions
  • Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are stored encrypted on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • Voice data is processed locally or by secure third-party APIs

6. Data Retention

  • Voice Data: Not retained - processed and immediately discarded
  • Conversation History: Stored locally until app is closed
  • Settings: Stored locally until app is uninstalled
  • Server Storage: We do not store any user data on our servers

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your conversation history in the app
  • Delete: Clear conversations or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Opt-out: Disable ads by purchasing Premium version
  • Control: Manage permissions in your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

Talkertive is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
